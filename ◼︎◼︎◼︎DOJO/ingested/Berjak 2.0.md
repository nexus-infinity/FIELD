# Berjak 2.0
This comprehensive integration plan seamlessly integrates the historical Berjak systems with contemporary implementations. Key aspects include:

**Historical Foundation (2007):**

- **Original Backbone Process:** Conceived during the MBA program.
- **Microsoft Dynamics CRM Implementation:** Proven real-world enterprise architecture.
- **Complete Trading Workflow System:** Implemented.

**Core Business Processes:**

- **Customer Verification and Management:** Ensures compliance and customer satisfaction.
- **Trade Lead Processing:** Identifies and develops potential trading opportunities.
- **Agent Commission Management:** Handles agent commissions and compensation.
- **Bid/Offer Price Negotiation:** Facilitates competitive pricing strategies.
- **Contract Finalisation:** Finalises contracts and ensures compliance.
- **Business Operations (Shipping, Insurance, Treasury):** Manages various business operations.
- **Claims Management:** Processes and resolves customer claims.

**Modern Integration Architecture:**

- **Seven Chakra-based Layers:** Aligns business functions with specific chakra energies.
    - **Root (/):** Foundation - Customer verification, basic trading.
    - **Sacral (/business):** Flow - Trade processing, commission management.
    - **Solar (/operations):** Power - Active trading, contract management.
    - **Heart (/operations/business):** Integration - Business operations.
    - **Throat (/network):** Communication - Claims, relationship management.
    - **Third Eye (/network/vision):** Intelligence - Analytics.
    - **Crown (/field):** Transcendence - FIELD system integration.

**Technical Implementation:**

- **Modern Stack:** Utilises Next.js, TypeScript, and PostgreSQL.
- **Preserved Data Models:** Retains comprehensive data models from 2007.
- **Real-time Market Data Integration:** Integrates LME pricing data.
- **Document Imaging and Correspondence:** Implements document imaging and correspondence capabilities.
- **Advanced Analytics and Visualisation:** Utilises advanced analytics and visualisation tools.
