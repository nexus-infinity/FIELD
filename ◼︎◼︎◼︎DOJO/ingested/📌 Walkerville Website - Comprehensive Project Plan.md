🔹 **Project Overview**

The **Walkerville Website** is a **multi-layered digital infrastructure** designed to:
	•	📜 **Store verified legal & advocacy documents securely.**
	•	🏛 **Empower individuals with automated legal processes.**
	•	🔗 **Leverage blockchain for verifiable, tamper-proof records.**
	•	🧠 **Integrate AI (Tata AI) for structured data insights.**
	•	💾 **Support cost-efficient, decentralised storage solutions.**

This **future-proof platform** will **combine Web3, AI, and legal automation** into a seamless, user-friendly system.

🔹 **Core System Components**

1️⃣ **Document Processing & Verification**

🔲 **Users upload legal, investigative, or advocacy documents.**
🔲 **AI-driven NLP extracts key metadata, dates, case references.**
🔲 **Documents are assigned a unique cryptographic hash.**

2️⃣ **Blockchain & Decentralized Storage**

🔲 **Documents are stored off-chain using IPFS/Arweave/Filecoin.**
🔲 **Their cryptographic hash is recorded on-chain for verification.**
🔲 **Users can prove document authenticity using the blockchain.**

3️⃣ **AI-Assisted Legal Case Management**

🔲 **Tata AI cross-references legal precedent & evidence.**
🔲 **Guides users on next steps in legal disputes.**
🔲 **Automates report generation (e.g., Statements of Claim, Affidavits).**

4️⃣ **User Authentication & Role Management**

🔲 **Secure user logins (OAuth, NextAuth, Web3 wallets).**
🔲 **Role-based access control (public, lawyer, admin).**
🔲 **User-owned data sovereignty.**

5️⃣ **Public Advocacy & Case Awareness**

🔲 **Social & legal action campaigns linked to cases.**
🔲 **Automated email, petition, and outreach tools.**
🔲 **Community engagement through secure channels.**

🔹 **Technology Stack**

1️⃣ **Backend**

✅ **PostgreSQL** - Relational database for structured legal cases.
✅ **MongoDB** - NoSQL for AI-enhanced data processing.
✅ **Blockchain** - Ethereum L2 (Polygon PoS) for document verification.
✅ **IPFS / Arweave / Filecoin** - Decentralized storage of full documents.
✅ **FastAPI / Node.js** - API for frontend-backend communication.

2️⃣ **Frontend**

✅ **Next.js** - Server-side rendering for dynamic web applications.
✅ **React** - Modular UI components for user-friendly interaction.
✅ **TailwindCSS** - Styling for responsive, accessible design.

3️⃣ **AI & Data Processing**

✅ **Tata AI** - NLP for document insights, evidence tracking.
✅ **OCR (Optical Character Recognition)** - Extracts text from PDFs/images.
✅ **Machine Learning** - Legal case prediction, document classification.
